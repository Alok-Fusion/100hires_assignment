# Nathan Gotch - YouTube Research

**Video Title:** 7 SEO Predictions for 2026

**Speaker:** Nathan Gotch

**Video Link:** https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=MimvQcTLzRA

---

## Overview

In this presentation, Nathan Gotch outlines seven predictions for the future of SEO based on more than a decade of industry experience. The discussion focuses on the growing role of AI search, declining organic click-through rates, user-generated content, AI visibility, and the evolution of SEO into a broader "Search Everywhere Optimization" strategy.

---

## Main Topics

### 1. Declining Organic CTR

AI Overviews and AI-generated search experiences continue reducing clicks to traditional search results. Nathan predicts that organic CTR for many informational queries will fall below 10%.

### 2. AI-First Search Experiences

Google, ChatGPT, Gemini, and Perplexity are increasingly becoming the primary interface for information discovery. Traditional search indexes will remain important but function mainly as retrieval systems behind AI-generated answers.

### 3. User-Generated Content

Platforms such as Reddit, Wikipedia, YouTube, and niche forums are becoming valuable sources for AI-generated responses because they provide unique, experience-based content.

### 4. Traditional SEO Still Matters

Technical SEO, site crawlability, indexing, structured content, and strong website foundations continue to improve visibility within both search engines and AI systems.

### 5. Social & Video Platforms

Nathan predicts that YouTube, LinkedIn, Reddit, TikTok, and other social platforms will become increasingly important for informational searches as users diversify where they consume content.

### 6. AI Spam Detection

As AI-generated spam increases, platforms like ChatGPT and Google are expected to strengthen quality controls and spam filtering systems.

### 7. Search Everywhere Optimization

SEO is evolving beyond ranking webpages. Businesses should optimize visibility across Google Search, AI assistants, YouTube, local search, Reddit, LinkedIn, and other discovery platforms.

---

## Key Takeaways

* AI search is reducing traditional website traffic.
* Visibility across multiple platforms is becoming essential.
* High-quality, experience-based content outperforms mass-produced AI content.
* User-generated content has growing influence on AI-generated answers.
* Technical SEO remains a critical foundation.
* Future SEO success depends on adapting to AI-driven search behavior.

Nathan Gotch combines practical SEO experience with forward-looking AI search strategies. His concept of "Search Everywhere Optimization" aligns closely with the evolving landscape of AI-powered SEO and provides actionable guidance for businesses adapting to AI-first search experiences.
